About Myself

Born in Naples in 1989, I started programming at High School when I was 16. After 2 years spent in studying various technologies (with a particular focus on OOP) I decided to join K-Makers S.r.l. and in 2009 to attend a training course in Java and related technologies. Since then, I have never stopped programming. After 5 years of working in Italian IT Industry, I moved to London looking for new challenges and opportunities of growth.

After changing two companies I decided to join 7digital and since July 2016 I'm part of the Interactive Radio Team based in London Moorgate's offices.

I'm very passionate about coding and learning new technologies but, aside from I also enjoy spending time with my friends, attending concerts and events, playing sports and salsa.
