About Myself

Since July 2014 I had the opportunity to learn and grow in 2 British companies but the plan nowadays is to find the definitive company where I'll be able to settle and create my future.
I'm very passionate in coding and learning new technologies but despite this I like to enjoy my friends, being social and play sports.
